Spaghetti Sauce Recipe

Description

This is a simple spaghetti sauce recipe that the anyone can enjoy. Made of less than 10 ingredients that can be found in most households. A recipe so delicious you won't believe it was this easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef (ground turkey or chicken can be used instead)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp of minced garlic
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 30oz cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp of Italian seasoning
  • salt, if desired, for seasoning
  • black pepper, if desired, for seasoning

Instructions

  1. In a large pot add the olive oil, when the oil is heated, add minced garlic and onion and sautee till brown, then add bell pepper and ground beef and cook until beef is brown.
  2. Drain the oil from pot and add crushed tomatoes into the pot.
  3. Add the Italian seasoning (and salt and pepper, if desired) in the pot, stir and cook for 30 minutes at medium heat.
  4. Wait for pot to cool for 10 minutes. Now the spaghetti sauce is ready to be served. Enjoy!
