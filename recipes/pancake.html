Creamy Pancake Recipe

Description

This is a pancake recipe that takes the boring and plain instructions at the back of pancake mixes and creates something new, fluffy, creamy, and delicious with it. Has very few ingredients and makes better pancakes than any restaurant, making you never have to eat out for breakfast again.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of pancake mix
  • 5 tbsps of olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup of milk
  • syrup, as topping

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl add all the pancake mix, eggs, heavy whipping cream milk and 3 tbsps of olive oil, mix together until mixture has a smooth and even consistency.
  2. In a large frying pan, add the remaininig olive oil and wait until heated, then with a scoop add the pancake mixture to the pan.
  3. Wait for two minutes and then flip the pancake on the other side. Wait another two minutes and take the pancake out of the pan and into a plate for it to cool off.
  4. Repeat the 3rd step until pancke mixture is finished. Then you have ready to eat pancakes that you can top with syrup. Enjoy.
