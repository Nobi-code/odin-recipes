Chili Recipe

Description

This is a quick and easy chili recipe that the whole family can enjoy. Made of ingredients that are widely available, and when finished will make your family believe that you are a professional chef.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef (ground turkey or chicken can be used instead)
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp of minced garlic
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 30oz cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 15oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 15oz can of black beans
  • 1 15oz can of red kidney beans
  • 1 Tbsp of dark chili powder
  • 1 tbsp of cumin powder
  • 1 tsp of smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp of cayenne pepper
  • salt, if desired, for seasoning
  • black pepper, if desired, for seasoning

Instructions

  1. In a large pot add the olive oil, when the oil is heated, add minced garlic and onion and sautee till brown, then add bell pepper and ground beef and cook until beef is brown.
  2. Drain the oil from pot and add crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, black beans and red kidney beans into the pot.
  3. Add all the seasoning in the pot, stir and cook for 30 minutes at medium heat.
  4. Wait for pot to cool for 10 minutes. Now the chili is ready to be served. Enjoy!
